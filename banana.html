POTASSIUM


Potassium (K) is a metal with an atomic number of nineteen. It is essential for the health of both plants and animals.

It is silver immediately after being cut, but quickly tarnishes. It is the seventh most abundant element on Earth.

Potassium is an important element found in bananas and many other foods. A depletion of potassium from the body can lead to hypokalemia, which can be fatal.
