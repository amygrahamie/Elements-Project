ARGON


Argon (Ar) is one of the noble gases and has an atomic number of eighteen, meaning it has eighteen protons in the nucleus of an atom of argon.

Argon is the most abundant and cheapest of the noble gases in the Earth's atmosphere. It was also the first noble gas discovered.

Incandescent lightbulbs are filled with argon. Argon is used to create neon lighting as it burns the colour blue.
